Rules

  • Any live cell with more than 3 neighbours dies due to overpopulation
  • Any live cell with 0 neighbours dies due to loneliness
  • Any dead cell with exactly 2 neighbours is reborn

Controls

  • Click hold and drag the mouse to move the field. Optionally use keyboard arrow keys
  • Toggle cells by pressing the Left Mouse Button. Optionally: move to cell with keyboard and press Enter Key
  • Press the space bar key to pause simulation
  • Press the Help button on the top right to toggle this help on or off
